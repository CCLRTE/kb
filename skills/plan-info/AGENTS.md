# Contents

- `SKILL.md` – workflow for creating and evolving durable knowledge-base plans.
- `references/structure.md` – adaptable plan frontmatter and section contract.
- `agents/openai.yaml` – agent-runner display metadata.

# Guidelines

- Keep the skill portable across repositories and independent of a specific product taxonomy.
- Require outcome, status, constraints, ordered work, and verification while allowing small plans to stay small.
- Evolve one plan in place; do not prescribe satellite progress or review files.
- Keep examples compatible with Obsidian, ordinary Markdown, and the installed `info` command.
